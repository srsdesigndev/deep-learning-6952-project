## Poster Sections

Using the provided template or a poster template of your choice with my approval, your task is to make a poster with the following sections:

### Background
Give a brief overview of what problem/domain/task your project is about, as well as your motivation for doing this project.

### Research Questions
State your two research questions (classification and regression), with each one explicitly labeled as such.

### Dataset
Give a brief overview of your dataset(s), including:
- 2-4 of the column names/types
- The train/test splits
- Your source for the dataset
- (Optionally) a visualization that illustrates some aspect of your dataset (e.g., distribution of values for the target value/class)

*If you got your data from scraping or an API, you can talk about that here.*

### Data Preparation
List any preprocessing steps that you had to do to get the data into a "usable" state, if you needed to.

### Classification Method
List the hyperparameters (e.g., layer sizes, activation functions, optimizer, loss function) of the networks you used to attempt to answer the classification RQ, as well as the methods of performance evaluation (e.g., F₁-score, precision).

*This can include a simple visualization of your network pipeline as well (can refer to the Lecture 18 notes for an example of what they can look like).*

### Classification Results
Present the numerical results (i.e., evaluation) of your classification model that you described in the methods section, including:
- A comparison of the different architectures and the baseline you chose
- Point out the one that performed best
- Provide a more detailed set of results for that model
- A brief conclusion statement that attempts to answer your classification RQ

*It may be helpful/worthwhile here to include a visualization such as a loss curve or confusion matrix.*

### Regression Method
List the hyperparameters (e.g., layer sizes, activation functions, optimizer, loss function) of the networks you used to attempt to answer the regression RQ, as well as the methods of performance evaluation (e.g., RMSE, MAE, MSE).

*This can include a simple visualization of your network pipeline as well (can refer to the Lecture 18 notes for an example of what they can look like).*

### Regression Results
Present the numerical results (i.e., evaluation) of your regression model that you described in the methods section, including:
- A comparison of the different architectures and the baseline you chose
- Point out the one that performed best
- Provide a more detailed set of results for that model
- A brief conclusion statement that attempts to answer your regression RQ

*It may be helpful/worthwhile here to include a visualization such as a loss curve.*

### Future Work
Briefly outline **two possible directions** for future work related to this project, either if you were to continue it or if someone else wanted to. These can be based on your results or further questions that you thought about as you were completing the project.
